Getting Started with Moana

Quick setup guide to start mining RavenCoin

1

Download & Extract

Download Moana.zip and extract it to a folder of your choice.

2

Add Miner

Download T-Rex or Rigel miner and place the .exe in the bin folder.

3

Configure

Run Moana.bat and enter your RVN wallet address in the dashboard.

4

Start Mining

Click Start Mining in the dashboard and watch your hashrate!
